Clinical-Grade Prediction, Simplified

CardioSense applies an ensemble Voting Classifier combining Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and AdaBoost — all trained on a balanced, real-world clinical dataset. The system analyses 16 key clinical and lifestyle indicators to generate a personalised risk score with actionable recommendations.

Trained on 10,000 real patient records
SMOTE-balanced dataset for fair predictions
4 ensemble models compared and combined
Personalised recommendations for each user
Built with Python, Flask, and scikit-learn
